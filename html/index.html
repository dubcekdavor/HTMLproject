Mi 9 Lite

Xiaomi Mi 9 Lite smartphone was launched in September 2019. The phone comes with a 6.39-inch display with a resolution of 1080x2340 pixels at a pixel density of 403 pixels per inch (ppi).

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Xiaomi Mi 9 Lite on the rear packs a 48-megapixel primary camera; a second 8-megapixel camera and a third 2-megapixel camera. It sports a 32-megapixel camera on the front for selfies, with an f/2.0 aperture.

Mi 9T

Mi 9T smartphone was launched on 12th June 2019. The phone comes with a 6.39-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080x2340 pixels and an aspect ratio of 19.5:9.

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Mi 9T on the rear packs a 48-megapixel primary camera with an f/1.75 aperture and a pixel size of 1.6-micron; a second 8-megapixel camera with an f/2.4 aperture and a pixel size of 1.12-micron and a third 13-megapixel camera with an f/2.4 aperture and a pixel size of 1.12-micron. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It sports a 20-megapixel camera on the front for selfies, with an f/2.2 aperture and a pixel size of 0.8-micron.

Mi 9 Pro

Xiaomi Mi 9 Pro 5G smartphone was launched on 24th September 2019. The phone comes with a 6.39-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080x2340 pixels at a pixel density of 403 pixels per inch (ppi) and an aspect ratio of 19.5:9.

Connectivity options on the Xiaomi Mi 9 Pro 5G include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, USB Type-C, Wi-Fi Direct, 3G, and 4G (with support for Band 40 used by some LTE networks in India). Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, compass/ magnetometer, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and fingerprint sensor. The Xiaomi Mi 9 Pro 5G supports face unlock.
